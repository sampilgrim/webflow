Our Activities

After trading more than USD 1bn clean power and emissions, Laurent Segalen and his partners founded Megawatt-X in 2013. Laurent Segalen has been awarded the titles of Renewable Energy Leader of the Year 2024" by EnergyBox and the Cititech Top50 voices in Energy and Commodities Award 2024.

London-based Megawatt-X has one mission: enabling the funding of the Energy Transition.

We invest in strategic sub sectors of the Energy Infrastructure.

Environmental Digital Markets

Megawatt-X was the first platform to list more than 15GW of wind and solar assets for investors to allocate capital. Then we seeded Zeigo, a ground breaking digital PPA platform that we ended up selling to Schneider Electric in 2022.

We are now fully invested into a new platform Renewabl, the premier market place to track and trade green electrons 24/7 under the rules of EnergyTag.

Energy behind the meter

As early investors in Febesol, a leading German rooftop solar installer, we helped drive its remarkable 700% growth, culminating in a successful sale to Thermondo in 2024.

Now, we have reinvested the capital into a new venture Abloco Energy dedicated to leasing mobile batteries. This operation includes a co-investment with Conquest Fund and a strategic partnership with the award-winning platform Skoon for digital management and logistics.

Interconnectors

After successfully arranging the Series A for a new Sub Sea interconnector, MaresConnect, between Ireland and GB, Megawatt-X is now considering longer distances.

Better technologies, the need for more Energy Security and the necessity to balance intermittent sources of renewable energy are very potential drivers for the development of transcontinental cables. Megawatt-X is at the forefront of those developments with NATO-L.

Knowledge

Megawatt-X co-hosts the Redefining Energy podcast, ranked as one the top 3 Energy podcast according to Google.

Launched in 2018, the podcast has been downloaded more than 2 million times and has gained multiple awards over the years.
